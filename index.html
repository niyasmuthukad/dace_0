Green Worms

Revolutionizing Waste Management through Social Entrepreneurship

Founded: 2014 | Location: Kerala, India

Founder: Jabir Karat




TEAM TECHNO FLIP: Bhoomija M, Thanha Noorudheen, A Harikrishna,Muhammed Niyas, P D Nivedh

The Problem & The Solution

  • The Crisis: 90% of plastic waste ends in landfills or oceans due to a lack of "last-mile" infrastructure.
  • The Solution (Social Procurement): Green Worms formalizes the informal sector, partnering with women's Self-Help Groups.
  • Methodology: Strict "Source-Level Segregation" to ensure high-quality material recovery and zero-waste events.

Empowering "Waste-Preneurs"

  • Beneficiaries: Over 3,000 women from Kudumbashree and Haritha Karma Sena networks.
  • Transformation: Converts "ragpicking" into dignified, salaried professions with safety gear and social respect.
  • Regional Focus: specifically targets vulnerable coastal communities and islands (Lakshadweep/Andamans).

Digital Innovation & Tech

  • 100% Traceability: Uses app-based tracking from Doorstep to Recycler, guaranteeing ethical sourcing.
  • FinTech Inclusion: Launched India’s first CBDC (Digital Rupee) pilot for sanitation workers.
  • Impact: Ensures transparent, direct wages and builds financial history for marginalized women.
